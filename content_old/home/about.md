# Biography

Neha Gupta is an anthropologist and recent postdoctoral fellow at Memorial University. Her research specialties include geovisualization and GIS, landscape and settlement archaeology and the archaeology of India. She recently launched [MINA | Map Indian Archaeology] (http://dngupta.github.io/mina.github.io), a public digital project to promote the archaeology of India and to encourage collaboration in the development of digital tools and technologies appropriate for archaeology. Her projects regularly center on the themes of colonial practices, web maps, Indigenous peoples and archaeology's relationship with society.
